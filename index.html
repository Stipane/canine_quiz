How to complete this Quiz

General Info

This quiz is a multiple choice quiz. You will be presented with a question and four possible answers. You must select an answer to proceed to the next question. You can only select one answer per question. Once you have completed the quiz, you will be presented with your score.

The quiz consists of (11) questions with a canine theme. The quiz is not for analytical purposes, it is a project for a web development course. No personal information is gathered/used.

To start the quiz, click 'Start Quiz' below.

Navigation

To navigate through the questions, click the 'Next" button once you answered the question. You must select an answer to enable the 'Next' button. You can click 'Previous Question' button to go back and change an answer.

Results

Results will displayed on screen once the last question is answered. The results will be shown as a fraction e.g. 11/15 and a percentage %. All your answers will be displayed along with the correct answers.

After Completion

Once the quiz has been completed and a score has been displayed, the window can be closed. No data is recorded. You can take the quiz as many times as you like. Just click 'Restart Quiz'.
